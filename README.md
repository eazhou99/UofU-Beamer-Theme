# UofU-Beamer-Theme

A University of Utah themed Beamer template for LaTeX presentations.

## Preview

Check out [`slide.pdf`](slide.pdf) to see the example presentation!

## Usage

1. Copy `UofU.sty` and `pic/` folder to your project
2. Add `\usepackage{UofU}` to your document preamble
3. Compile with pdfLaTeX or XeLaTeX

## Color Scheme

- **Utah Red**: #BE0000 (RGB: 190, 0, 0)

## University of Utah Logo Usage

### Official Logo Downloads

Official University of Utah logos require authentication to download:

- **UofU Employees/Students**: Log in with your uNID at [brand.utah.edu/secure/university-of-utah-logo-downloads](https://brand.utah.edu/secure/university-of-utah-logo-downloads/)
- **Non-employees**: Complete the [University Logo Request Form](https://brand.utah.edu/branding/logos/)

### Logo Usage Guidelines

Per [University of Utah Brand Guidelines](https://brand.utah.edu/branding/logos/):

- The logo must function as a unified mark â€” do not resize, rearrange, or alter components independently
- Maintain clear space equal to 1/4 the height of the Block U
- Minimum size: 0.5625 inches (print) or 100 pixels (web)

**Prohibited uses:**
- Distorting, stretching, skewing, or rotating
- Changing approved colors
- Adding outlines, glows, shadows, or gradients
- Using as backgrounds or watermarks
- Incorporating into other logos

Only use official University-provided logo files. Custom arrangements or derivatives are prohibited.
